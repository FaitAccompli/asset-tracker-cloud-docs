Device-to-Cloud ProtocolÂ¶

This sections documents the protocol and the considerations we made when selecting the device-to-cloud protocol for the Cat Tracker.

Note

Communication is the most important aspect to optimize for when developing an ultra-low-power product because initiating and maintaining network connection is relatively expensive compared to other device operations (for example reading a sensor value). It is therefore recommended to invest a reasonable amount of time to revisit the principles explained here and customize them to your specific needs. The more the modem-uptime can be reduced and the smaller the total transferred amount if data becomes, the longer will your battery and your data contingent last.
